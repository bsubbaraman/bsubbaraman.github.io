I’m Blair, a PhD candidate in the department of Human-Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington. I work in the Machine Agency and am advised by Nadya Peek. My work explores physical automation as a creative medium. From artists to plant biologists, I build systems with & for domain experts to creatively engage computer-controlled machines, and examine how the use of machines in nontraditional contexts can inform the development of future systems. In this process I am committed to developing open-source hardware and software to enable artistic, scientific, and other creative exploration.

I will be applying to tenure-track faculty positions in Fall 2025.
